Book me for a Design Day!

If your studio needs motion graphics, logo animation, or help editing video footage into polished social media content, I offer one-day freelance bookings. I work alongside your team, onsite or remotely, for a focused eight-hour block. This service covers tasks your studio may not have the time or skills to tackle, including finishing reels, animating branding elements, and creating hero sections or product showreels.

Design Days are perfect for studios with raw footage waiting to be turned into something post-worthy, or anyone wanting to offer interactive motion graphics to impress their clients. Just shoot me a message to discuss your project or reserve a date. Note that I currently only work through temp agencies in Belgium.
