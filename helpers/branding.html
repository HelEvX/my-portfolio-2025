Recommendation
Option 1 (Retro Digital Orange + Teal) is my top pick for your glitch/VHS theme. Here's why:

✓ Maintains your orange preference but with more retro character
✓ Teal secondary is quintessential 90s digital aesthetic
✓ The "VHS black" (#0f0f23) has that slight blue undertone that prevents it from being flat
✓ All colors have the right "analog imperfection" - punchy but not pure
✓ Perfect contrast ratios for accessibility
✓ Avoids obvious brand associations (not Fanta orange, not Instagram teal)

Option 3 (VHS Orange + Synthwave Blue) is great if you want more of that classic synthwave aesthetic - closer to your original orange but with that iconic 80s-90s blue complement.
