Customisable Interstitial Cards & Lower Thirds for Foil Arms and Hog's Top Ten Sketch Review

Sketch comedy trio Foil Arms & Hog, who put out weekly sketches on YouTube, premiered a 'top ten review' of their own favourite work to close the decade in 2019. I was a new fan of theirs then, but by the end of 2020 I had already been hired to do motion graphics for them so I suggested they might like to have custom-made titles for their 2020 review episode.

I only had a couple of days, so I presented a fairly 'minimal' set of lower thirds motion graphics template (.mogrt) to choose from and they used those for 2020 and 2021. But for 2022 I approached them again, offering them a different direction. I felt my skills had progressed a lot since my first attempt and they should benefit from it, and besides it never hurts to try. They've been using this version ever since.

The main strenghts of the new edition:

  • A more playful, eye-catching 'sketchy' visual style, built in Illustrator.
  • More options for customisation, among which ten different 'pig piece drops'.
  • Thorough testing to accommodate for title lenght differences.
  • Future-proofing artwork in 6K with the option to scale down.

I'll talk more about my process, design decisions and motion techniques in the dedicated blog post.
